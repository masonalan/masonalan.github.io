tyOS is primarily a music & technology company, although we also take design comissions (no refunds).

Started by Tyson Malone, an international man of mystery, tyOS quickly became the industry standard for music
technologies for the better part of the 1980s. No one is sure how tyOS gained so much success so rapidly. Some
say it was Malone's close ties with Steve Jobbs (founder of Apple). Others claim it was his unmatched charisma and creative eye.

All the success got to Malone, and after picking up a nasty coke habit, he ran his Countach off the Brooklyn Bridge.
Fortunately, he was able to leave a legacy strong enough for Bjarne Stroustrup to list Malone as one of his primary inspirations
for writing C++. Malone and his vision are survived through his disciple James Pickering, who is the current CEO of tyOS.
